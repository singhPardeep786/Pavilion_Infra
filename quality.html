Quality

At Pavilion Infra Group, quality is of great importance to us and rightly so, all our projects are not just high in quality but also inspire many. From planning to delivery, we are quality-centric and associate with those who believe in the same. Quality builds reputation and all our esteemed board of director have been inclined towards quality always. Quality has always driven us further and we wish to be synonymous with quality always.

EHS Policy

At Pavilion Infra , we vow to meet all applicable environment, health and safety, legal and other requirements and to establish EHS objectives, targets and report results on our performance. We also make sincere efforts to design our products for sustainable use of earth's resources and to operate consistently with our EHS policy. We also evaluate performances by monitoring results through effective management reviews. We empower employees to effectively implement EHS policy.
